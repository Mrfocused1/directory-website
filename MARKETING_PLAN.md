# BuildMy.Directory — Marketing Plan

**Date:** April 2026
**Product:** BuildMy.Directory — Turn social media content into searchable, monetizable directories
**Core tagline:** "Your content, fully searchable."

---

## 1. Executive Summary

BuildMy.Directory transforms fragmented social media content (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) into beautiful, searchable, SEO-optimized directories — fully automated in under 5 minutes. Creators get transcription, auto-categorization, custom domains, email newsletters, and analytics without any manual work.

This plan outlines a phased go-to-market strategy targeting content creators, educators, and agencies, leveraging product-led growth, creator partnerships, and content marketing to reach **1,000 paying customers within 12 months**.

## 5. Growth Loops

### Loop 1: Powered-by Badge (Viral)
Every free and Creator directory includes a "Powered by BuildMy.Directory" footer badge. When a creator shares their directory, their audience sees the badge → clicks → signs up → creates their own directory → repeats.

**Optimization:** Make the badge visually appealing. Track click-through rate. A/B test badge copy ("Make yours free" vs "Build your directory").

### Loop 2: Email Newsletter (Retention + Discovery)
Creators send digests to their subscribers via BuildMy.Directory. Subscribers engage with the directory. Footer includes "Get your own directory" CTA.

### Loop 3: Content Request Board (Engagement)
Visitors request content → creator sees demand → creates content → shares directory again. Keeps the directory alive and growing.

### Loop 4: SEO (Compounding)
Each directory generates SEO-optimized pages with JSON-LD, sitemaps, and canonical URLs. As directories rank, they bring organic traffic to both the creator and to BuildMy.Directory.
